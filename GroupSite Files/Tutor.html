Advising Overview

Academic advising is a collaborative process—we'll partner with you to plan your personal, academic, and career goals and help you succeed as a student in the College of Science and Engineering.

Advisors are here to guide you with course planning, exploring majors/minors, monitoring your progress, and connecting you to campus resources.
